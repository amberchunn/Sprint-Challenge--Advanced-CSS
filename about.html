Remember the Wonder?

NASA’s Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland, released its third issue of a new online news magazine for kids, teens and educators called “NASA Next.”

The third issue, for winter 2017, is available for download. The magazine seeks to engage kids and teens in NASA science, and serve as a resource for educators. This issue’s theme is “Dreaming Big.” At NASA, we dream big every day. Our ambitions take us to new heights as we launch spacecraft and make extraordinary discoveries.

“I want to encourage readers that whether your dream is big or small, keep striving for it,” said NASA Next editor Sarah Schlieder. “Even the smallest of ambitions can have a big impact.”
